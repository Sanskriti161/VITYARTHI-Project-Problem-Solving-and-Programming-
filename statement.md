# Problem Statement & Scope

## Problem Statement
Managing personal finances manually can be frustrating and may not always be correct. Users especially students in college need a secure, reliable banking system to create accounts, perform transactions, and maintain records. A digital banking application provides a safe platform for account management with security through its PIN verification and transaction history like features.

## Scope of the Project

The project includes:
- Banking Account creation with secure PIN authentication
- Account login and verification
- Deposit and withdrawal of money
- Real-time balance checking
- Complete transaction history provided
- Account information management
- Security features like PIN verification

## Target Users
- Individuals especially college students wanting to manage their finances.
- Students learning banking concepts.
